IPAD New Features

There are two models of the new iPad Pro. The 11-inch model starts at $799, and the 12.9-inch model starts at $1,099. We're reviewing the larger model, which is the only one with the new XDR Mini LED display. They both come in gray or silver. The 12.9-inch model measures 11.04 by 8.46 by 0.25 inches (HWD), and its weight of just 1.5 pounds belies its speed and power. You're unlikely to pay just $1,099 for this tablet. That price is for a 128GB model, and you'll probably overrun that with large media files after a year or so. The Pro can use external storage, but in iPadOS, external drives are second-class citizens that can't be used directly by some apps, so you want that internal storage. Hard drive options include 256GB (for an extra $100), 512GB ($300 more), 1TB (add $700), and 2TB (add $1,100; totaling two times the entry-level cost).
